About This Treatment

Hot Stone Therapy is a deeply relaxing massage treatment that uses smooth, heated basalt stones placed on specific points of the body. The heat from the stones penetrates deep into muscles, relieving tension and promoting relaxation.

Perfect Temperature

Stones heated to optimal temperature for maximum therapeutic benefit

Deep Relaxation

Combines heat therapy with massage techniques for profound relaxation

Natural Materials

Using natural basalt stones and premium essential oils

Benefits

Improves Circulation

Heat dilates blood vessels, improving blood flow

Reduces Stress

Deep relaxation helps alleviate mental tension

Better Sleep

Promotes restful sleep and overall well-being

Muscle Recovery

Relieves muscle soreness and speeds up recovery
