Frequently Asked Questions

When will my card be charged?

Your card will be charged immediately upon booking confirmation. We use Stripe for secure payments.

What is your refund policy?

Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before appointment. 50% refund for cancellations within 24 hours.

What should I know before my visit?

To make the most of your experience, we recommend arriving 15-20 minutes before your appointment. This allows time to check in, change, and begin relaxing in our lounge. Please inform us of any allergies, medical conditions, or if you are pregnant when booking.

Can I change my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule anytime up to 24 hours before your appointment through your confirmation email.
